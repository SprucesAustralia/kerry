Our Commitment to a Spotless Clean

Here is a detailed checklist of the tasks we will perform during each weekly visit to your "small offices."

Office & Desk Areas

  • Vacuum Carpet Tiles: Using commercial-grade, HEPA-filter backpack vacuums to ensure deep cleaning and air quality.
  • Wipe Down Office Desks: Dust and wipe all accessible surfaces with microfiber cloths and a pH-neutral multi-surface cleaner.
  • Empty Bins: Replace liners and wipe down bin surfaces as needed.
  • Spot Clean Walls & Doors: Remove fingerprints and marks from light switches and door handles.

"Small Kitchenettes"

  • Clean Countertops & Sinks: Sanitize all surfaces using food-safe cleaning agents.
  • Wipe Appliance Exteriors: Clean the outside of microwaves, fridges, etc.
  • Mop Hard Floors: Using a two-bucket system and appropriate floor cleaner to ensure a streak-free shine.
  • Restock Consumables: (If supplied by you).

Entrances & General

  • Clean Entrance Glass: Ensure front door glass is free of prints and smudges.
  • Mop Accessibility Ramps: We will sweep and mop the "black leveler" wheelchair ramps at the entrances.
  • Final Inspection: A walkthrough to ensure everything meets our high standards before we leave.
